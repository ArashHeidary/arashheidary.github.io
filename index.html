Who Am I?

Hi I'm Arash Heidari. I currently work as a researcher at the Electronics Research Center (ERC) at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST). I have experience in Embedded Systems and Embedded Software Programming, like AVR microcontrollers, PIC microcontrollers, ARM etc. I have some experiences with IoT too and I've worked with Raspberry PI and Arduino and the related modules as well. Data Science is my other field of interest. I have some experience with Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Besides, I can work well with data. For example Tableau, R programming and SQL are the tools and software I can work with to mine, wrangle and analyze the data.

Furthermore, one of my interests is programming. So I know some of the programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, C/C++, etc. I have developed some smartphone application using ReactNative Library and some Desktop GUIs with Python and QT.

I have a Master Degree in Digital Electronic Systems and graduated as the best student of the year in my field of study from Iran University of Science and Technology where I have obtained my Bachelors as well.

I have many hobbies such as watching movies and series, reading books, etc., but learning new things is the most important hobby I have. Actually, that's the reason, why I have so many skills in many fields. Beside skills I have mentioned before and will tell more about them in the Skills section, I read books about physics, genetics and history. All these things besides, I am a good team-player, a fast learner, and an energetic person.
